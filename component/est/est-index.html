About Us

Welcome to the Royal Asian Restaurant!

We are located in heart of Jõgeva County at Suur 6. We offer delicious different kitchens’ cuisines such as Chinese, Thai, Indian & Nepali.

Our cuisines are made from fresh ingredients according to the original recipes.

We offer free basmati rice with Nepalese and Thai meat dishes and give 10 % discount to students and teachers with the ISIC and ITIC cards.

Delivery to home and take away are offered to our clients.

Daily offers M-F at 10-15: Day Special Lunch € 3.20 (with 1. Rice or 2. Noodles) and 3. Soup € 2 together with free lemon water.

Please contact with us in advance for major birthdays and celebrations.
